You have Power to Bring Joy.

The Joy Makers is a non-profit voluntary organization in india with an objective
of walfare and well-being of elderly people orphaned by their children and those
who are economically poor in our society.

A bit about us..

The Joy Makers believes to change the way pepole
treat at ageing in India. We enable aged people to lead active
lives through easy access to trusted information,
opportunities for productive ageing and health care support
services. The Joy Makers create an enlightnment society
in which elderly feel secure, confident and valued,
and can live without hope and dignity.
